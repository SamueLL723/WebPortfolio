Fabio Quartararo

Posted by Me on 27.11.2025

Fabio Quartararo, born on 20 April 1999 in Nice, France, is a French Grand Prix motorcycle racer competing in MotoGP for the Monster Energy Yamaha MotoGP Team, where he rides under the number 20. He is nicknamed "El Diablo" and made history as the first French rider to win the MotoGP World Championship, achieving this feat in 2021. Quartararo began his racing career at the age of four in his native France before moving to Spain to compete in the Promovelocidad Cup, where he won titles in the 50cc, 70cc, and 80cc classes.

MotoGP

Posted by Me on 27.11.2025

MotoGP™ is the world’s premier motorcycle racing championship, governed by the Fédération Internationale de Motocyclisme (FIM), with the full title of MotoGP™ being the FIM Grand Prix World Championship. It features the most talented riders on the planet, racing at 22 Grands Prix held annually across the globe, with events taking place in Asia, Europe, North America, South America, and Oceania. The championship is divided into three classes: MotoGP, Moto2, and Moto3, all using four-stroke engines.

UFC

Posted by Me on 27.11.2025

The UFC, or Ultimate Fighting Championship, is the world's leading professional mixed martial arts (MMA) organization, based in Las Vegas, Nevada. Founded in 1993 by Art Davie, Rorion Gracie, and Bob Meyrowitz, the UFC was initially conceived as a single-night tournament to determine the most effective martial art by pitting fighters from various disciplines—such as boxing, wrestling, jiu-jitsu, and kickboxing—against each other with minimal rules. Over time, it evolved from a controversial experimental event into a highly organized, sanctioned combat sport and a global sports brand, now producing over 40 live events annually. The organization is known for its eight-sided arena, the Octagon, and features fighters across multiple weight classes, governed by unified MMA rules, while also serving as a major media content company and the largest Pay-Per-View (PPV) event provider in the world.

Charles Oliveira da Silva

Posted by Me on 27.11.2025

Charles Oliveira da Silva, born on October 17, 1989, in the favela of Vicente de Carvalho, Guarujá, São Paulo, Brazil, is a Brazilian professional mixed martial artist and fourth-degree black belt in Brazilian jiu-jitsu. He is affectionately known as "do Bronxs" and currently competes in the UFC's Lightweight division, where he is a former UFC Lightweight Champion. Oliveira holds multiple UFC records, including the most submission wins (17), most finishes (21), and most performance bonuses (21) in the organization's history. He began training in Brazilian jiu-jitsu at age 12 under Roger Coelho, quickly achieving state and international titles before transitioning to MMA in 2007.

Will Tennyson

Posted by Me on 27.11.2025

Will Tennyson is a Canadian fitness and lifestyle YouTuber born on August 30, 1994, in Toronto, Canada. He gained popularity through his self-titled YouTube channel, which features content centered on fitness, food challenges, and lifestyle vlogs, with a focus on making health and wellness accessible and entertaining. His journey from being overweight in high school—weighing around 270 pounds—to achieving a fit physique has inspired many, and he openly shares his personal experiences, including his transformation and dedication to healthy living. He has a background in business, having earned a Bachelor of Business Administration from George Brown College and a Bachelor of Arts from the University of Guelph, and he is also an author, with his cookbook Bite Me scheduled for release on December 23, 2025. Will is known for his charismatic and humorous approach, and he has collaborated with brands like Gymshark and BPN-Bare Performance Nutrition. He is in a relationship with Kaitlyn, and his family includes his parents, Paul and Nancy Tennyson, and two sisters, Victoria (a lawyer) and Elizabeth (a neuroscience PhD graduate). His YouTube channel, launched on April 1, 2015, has amassed over 2.2 million subscribers and more than one billion views, with notable videos including fitness challenges and personal transformation stories.
